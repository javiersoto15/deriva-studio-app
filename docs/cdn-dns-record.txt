WHERE TO ADD

WHAT THIS DOES
--------------
Points the media.* subdomain at the Google Cloud HTTPS Load Balancer
that fronts the private GCS bucket `deriva-photos` with Cloud CDN.

After the record propagates (~5–30 min):
  1. The managed SSL cert finishes provisioning (auto)
  2. https://media.derivastudio.cl/v1/<slug>-<width>.<format> starts serving
  3. The cinematic landing's <picture> srcsets resolve correctly

  --project=deriva-studio-production \

curl -sI https://media.derivastudio.cl/v1/storefront-960.avif

             cache-control: public, max-age=31536000, immutable
